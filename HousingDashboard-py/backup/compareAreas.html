About SmartHousing

SmartHousing is a data-driven tool designed to empower students, families, and residents in Sheffield to make smarter housing decisions. Built as part of a collaborative software project, this platform leverages liveability data, cost of living, and green space access across neighborhoods — all visualized on an interactive map.

Our goal is to simplify the complex process of choosing where to live by providing intuitive filters and transparent insights. Users can:

  • Compare neighborhoods based on affordability and livability scores
  • Visualize access to green spaces using real geospatial data
  • Interact with a clean and responsive dashboard

This project reflects our commitment to user-centered design and practical innovation. It was developed with modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Leaflet.js, and open-source GeoJSON data.

Whether you're a student relocating to Sheffield or a resident exploring new areas, SmartHousing helps you find the right fit — intelligently and efficiently.
