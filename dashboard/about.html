Project Vision & Team Architecture

Engineering transparent recommendations for sustainable urban relocation.

Our Core Mission

We recognised how difficult it can be to find housing that is both affordable and genuinely livable, particularly for students and vulnerable individuals. Built during the Sheffield AI Hackathon, our web-based platform combines local rental pricing, green space accessibility, and socioeconomic data to help users make more informed housing decisions.

Through interactive visualisations and neighbourhood analysis tools, we transform complex local housing data into accessible insights that highlight affordability, environmental quality, and overall area suitability.
